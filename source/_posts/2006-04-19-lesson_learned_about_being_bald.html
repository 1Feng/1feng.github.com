--- layout: post title: Lesson learned about being bald ---

Sunscreen is your friend. It still doesn't hurt like a normal sunburn, but I have the pattern of a Pnuemo helmet on my head. Sunday I went out for a road ride in the middle of the day and didn't wear any sort of cap under my helmet. When I got out of the shower after the ride I noticed my head was awfully red, then it dawned on me. Oops...

In other news, I'm one ride away from having as many rides in 2006 as I managed in 2005 and hitting the 100k vertical feet mark. Tonight I'm climbing on the mountain bike, so I'm sure I'll get that in no problem.
