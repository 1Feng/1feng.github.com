--- layout: post title: Brown vs. Board of Education Anniversary ---

Today makes the 50th anniversary of Brown vs. Board of Education. On my way to lunch this afternoon, I listened to a portion of the Brown vs. Board of Education ceremony in Kansas. The governor of Kansas, Kathleen Sebelius, gave an excellent speech, with one exception. She called for continued action to insure that each child receives the quality education that they are entitled to but failed to explain how she planned on making that call to action a reality within Kansas. It's easy to say every child should receive the world's best education, it's a beast entirely different to explain how to go about that.
