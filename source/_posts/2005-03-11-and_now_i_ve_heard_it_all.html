--- layout: post title: And now I've heard it all... ---

Ok, just when you think you've heard everything from everybody, a tourist hits you out of the blue. I went to Gate's on Main today for lunch. I'm standing in line waiting to pay, and the guy behind me, who had been studying the menu for a little longer than normal stepped up to the counter and asked if they had any salads. It was all I could do to keep from chuckling. I did turn away from him so he couldn't seem me smiling.

Just out of curiosity, what part of the world do you go to a BBQ place and ask for a salad?
