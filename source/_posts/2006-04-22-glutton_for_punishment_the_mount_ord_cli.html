--- layout: post title: "Glutton for punishment: The Mount Ord Climb" ---

What can I say? I love climbing. Ok, well maybe love is a strong word, but I thoroughly enjoy climbing. Just not racing and climbing. I haven't worked those two into a good relationship yet, but I'm getting there.

So today me and a few other mountain bikers (read: crazy people) from Fountain Hills took off for Mount Ord. It was 5.75 miles to the top and took over an hour. That's right, an hour of going straight up.
