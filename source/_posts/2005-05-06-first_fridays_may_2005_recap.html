--- layout: post title: First Fridays May 2005 Recap ---

I'm back from First Fridays tonight. I don't feel like I know that many people in Kansas City, but I couldn't go 15 or 20 minutes without running into someone I knew. Weird how that works.

Anyhow, tonight was by far the best First Friday of the year. When I got back to my car at 9:30, the tempature was still 75! It was perfect weather. I took a ton of pictures. I'm uploading the 244 pictures I took between 5 and the time my battery finally ran out about 8:45. These are all raw images with no post-production on them, so keep that in mind. If I get the time over the weekend, I'll finish some of them up. There's also going to be a ton of pictures of the same people (probably the first 60 or 70). It was Judy's birthday, so I hung out with them and took a lot of pictures before venturing forth to the rest of the festivities.
