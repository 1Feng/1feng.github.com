I'm in the process of switching the majority of my hosting over to Dreamhost. There might be some outages on some of my sites while I go through this process, so I'm apologizing up front.

A shameless plus, cause I get some money from them too, but if you're looking for a good budget host with a lot of features, I'd definitely recommend them. If you sign up, be sure to put "TRAVIS50OFF" as the offer code and it'll give you $50 off of whatever hosting you sign up for.
