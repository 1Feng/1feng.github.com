I've launched www.MamboNews.info - one source for the latest news from Mamboland.

It's still really rough and only has the first parts of the layout in place. It is updated every five minutes with the latest from around Mamboland. If you have an RSS feed related to Mambo that you would like see included, please feel free to email it to. If you're a designer and would like to contribute logo, please email me!
