--- layout: post title: Ode to the Mr. Roadside Grass Cutter Guy ---

Most folks probably barely noticed them on the way in from the Northland, but the last few mornings there have been crews of workers cleaning up, cutting down, and generally "beautifying" the medians and shoulders of 169. Thanks to these hard working souls, the roadway now looks less like a jungle trail, and more like modern highway.

So here's to you... Mr. Roadside Grass Cutter Guy!
